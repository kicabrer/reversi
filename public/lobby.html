Get Started

  1. Begin by clicking "Let's Play!"
  2. Enter your preferred username and continue to Lobby.
  3. Send messages to other players in the Lobby.
  4. Invite another player to a game or accept an invitation from another player.

Once you invite another player to a game and they accept your invitation, or once you accept another player's invitation, the game page will load and you can begin playing.

Rules

Reversi is a 2-player strategy game on an 8x8 board using 64 tokens with different colors on each side. The game starts with 4 tokens placed in the 4 central squares of the board, with 2 of each color facing up and the same-colored tokens on a diagonal with each other.

Players take turns placing tokens on the board with their assigned color facing up. Tokens in the opponent's color that are bounded by the current player's color and the token just placed in a straight line are flipped into the current player's color.

The game ends when there are no further legal moves. The winner is the player with the most tokens in their assigned color on the board at the end of the game.

For detailed rules, please refer to Wikipedia's Reversi rules section.
